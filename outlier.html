OSINT & early-warning research concept

Outlier

Under development

An OPSEC-first platform concept for identifying weak signals, anomalies, and emerging risk in public data.

Possible applications

Situational awareness without unnecessary surveillance.

  • Detect unusual changes in public information environments.
  • Surface coordinated harassment, intimidation, or manipulation indicators.
  • Support watchdog, research, and civil-society analysis.
  • Prioritize explainable anomaly alerts over opaque scoring of individuals.
Design principles

OPSEC, proportionality, and civil-liberty boundaries.

Outlier is being framed around compartmentalization, data minimization, clear scope limits, explainability, and resistance to mission creep.

It is not presented as a finished service, operational surveillance system, or validated decision engine.
