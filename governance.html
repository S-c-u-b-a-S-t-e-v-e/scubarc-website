Governance & transparency

Strong research requires accountable institutions.

ScubaRC is building governance around fiduciary oversight, research integrity, conflict management, safety, and honest public reporting.

Board oversight

The Foundation board will hold fiduciary responsibility for charitable purpose, strategy, finances, and compliance.

Conflict controls

Related-party activity, compensation, licensing, and transactions with the LLC will require documentation and independent review.

Research integrity

Public claims should distinguish proposals, prototypes, evidence, limitations, and completed results.

Safety review

Potential misuse, privacy impact, operational sensitivity, and public harm should be considered before release or deployment.

Planned transparency

Documents and reporting to be published as they are finalized.

  • Foundation formation and federal tax-exemption status
  • Board and officer information appropriate for public disclosure
  • Bylaws and key governance policies
  • Conflict-of-interest and research-integrity policies
  • Annual summaries of programs, partnerships, and use of funds
Governance materials shown on this site are descriptions of intended structure until the relevant documents are formally adopted and published.
