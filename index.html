Veteran-founded public-interest research

Advanced research. Practical tools. Public benefit.

Scuba Research Collective develops responsible artificial intelligence, cognitive-security, advanced-sensing, and early-warning research intended to strengthen human safety, autonomy, and resilience.

Our purpose is to turn difficult ideas into useful, accountable work that serves the public—not only institutions or individuals with the greatest resources.

How we work

Evidence before claims.

ScubaRC separates research concepts, prototypes, public frameworks, and validated systems. Each project is labeled by its actual development status.

Human benefit before novelty.Technology should improve real lives.
Access beyond wealth.Public value should not depend on purchasing power.
Security without exploitation.Defensive capability should not become coercive capability.
Transparency with responsible limits.We publish enough to support scrutiny while protecting sensitive IP and methods.
Institutional structure

One mission, two distinct entities.

Scuba Research Collective LLC supports operational, commercial, contracting, and product activity. Scuba Research Collective Foundation is being formed separately for charitable, educational, scientific, and public-benefit work.
