Foundation status

Scuba Research Collective Foundation is in formation.

The Foundation is being established as a separate Virginia nonprofit corporation for charitable, educational, scientific, and public-benefit programs.

Current status: Federal recognition under section 501(c)(3) has not yet been granted. Contributions are not currently represented as tax-deductible.

Governance intent

Independence, transparency, and documented boundaries.

The Foundation will maintain its own governing documents, board oversight, financial records, bank accounts, contracts, and conflict-of-interest procedures. Any relationship with the LLC will be documented, reviewed, and structured to protect the Foundation's charitable purpose.
