Quantum & advanced-sensing research

WAVE & FRQTRS

Concept validation

Research concepts exploring advanced sensing, magnetometry, signal extraction, inspection, and forensic traceability.

WAVE

A research program examining whether advanced sensing and multimodal analysis can improve inspection of welds and critical infrastructure.

FRQTRS

A research concept examining whether environmental, residue, or material signatures can support more precise forensic traceability.

Research posture

Rigorous limits before technological hype.

These concepts require substantial validation across physics, engineering, data quality, sensor limitations, field conditions, cost, and comparison with established inspection or forensic methods. They are not presented as finished products.
