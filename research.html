Research at the edge of AI, cognition, security, and resilience.

Scuba Research Collective publishes public-facing research on cognitive security, information warfare, AI security, cyber threat intelligence, quantum sensing, and emerging technology risk. Sensitive implementation details, proprietary IP, code, and operational methods are maintained offline.

Research lanes

Defensive research focused on protecting human judgment, organizational decision-making, and public trust in contested information environments.

Frameworks for understanding influence operations, narrative manipulation, credibility laundering, synthetic personas, and AI-amplified persuasion.

Practical research on secure AI adoption, analyst augmentation, RAG workflows, evaluation, governance, guardrails, and operational trust.

Resilience & Recovery Systems

Non-clinical research into trauma-aware support tools, veteran resilience, and ethical AI systems designed to reduce friction between support sessions.
