Cognitive-security research framework

Zero-Sum MDIB

Under development

A defensive framework for studying disinformation ecosystems, narrative propagation, credibility laundering, synthetic personas, and coordinated influence activity.

Research objective

Make influence analysis more transparent and auditable.

  • Identify narrative families and amplification patterns.
  • Trace how claims move across platforms and communities.
  • Separate evidence from inference and confidence from certainty.
  • Support defenders, researchers, journalists, and civil-society organizations.
Boundary

Defensive analysis, not persuasion.

The framework is not intended for covert influence, domestic targeting, mass manipulation, individualized persuasion, or black-box scoring of people.

Public materials emphasize doctrine, transparency, ethics, and defensive use. Sensitive methods and implementation details remain offline.
